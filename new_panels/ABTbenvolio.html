Who is Benvolio?

Benvolio is our mascot!
He used to be a regular red panda, living in a bamboo forest along with his firend and family! Once upon a time, Benvolio, while searching for food, stumbled upon a laptop, and like any normal red panda, he started learning to code! He found his passion for programming and now he found a job in helping people with their code!

Benvolio's favorite snack is bamboo, so make sure you feed him from time to time! (You get one piece of bamboo for every 30 minutes spent on iTECify!)
